# Railway Overview

**Location:** Fairbourne, Gwynedd, Wales  
**Line:** Fairbourne Railway (Fairbourne & Barmouth Railway)

The line runs approximately 2 miles (3 km) between Fairbourne village and Barmouth Ferry along the Mawddach estuary.

## Stations
- Fairbourne Station  
- Penrhyn Station  
- Golf Halt  
- Barmouth Ferry

## Gauge
12¼ in (311 mm)

## History
The line began as a horse-drawn tramway in 1895 and was later converted to miniature steam operation in 1916. It was re-gauged to 12¼ in in the 1980s and continues as a heritage attraction today.
